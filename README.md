📚 StudyNest – AI-Powered Focus & Learning Ecosystem

StudyNest is an AI-driven, gamified learning platform designed to improve students’ focus, understanding, and motivation. Unlike traditional productivity tools that only track time, StudyNest tracks attention, learning quality, and consistency, transforming passive study into an active and measurable experience.

🚀 Problem Statement

Modern study habits suffer from a fragmented learning ecosystem:

❌ Low engagement due to monotonous routines

❌ Constant distractions with no real-time accountability

❌ Lack of feedback on conceptual clarity and answer quality

❌ Poor preparation for viva and oral examinations

Most tools measure how long students study, but not how well they study.

💡 Our Solution

StudyNest is an integrated AI-powered platform that combines:

Intelligent focus management

AI-based learning evaluation

Gamified motivation

It ensures that students remain focused, understand concepts deeply, and stay motivated consistently.

🏠 Home Page (Control Center)

The Home Page acts as the central dashboard of StudyNest.

Features:

Real-time focus score

Session duration and streaks

Distraction history

Earned rewards and XP

Impact: Gives students instant awareness of their productivity and creates accountability from the start.

⏱️ Smart Pomodoro System

StudyNest uses a structured Pomodoro-based time management system.

How it works:

Focused study intervals followed by short breaks

Prevents burnout and cognitive overload

Encourages consistency over cramming

Impact: Helps students maintain long-term discipline and sustain deep focus.

👁️ AI Attention Tracker (Core Innovation)

The attention tracker uses computer vision to detect real-time focus.

Tech Used:

TensorFlow

MediaPipe Face & Iris Tracking

Webcam-based gaze and head movement detection

How it works:

Detects when a student looks away or gets distracted

Provides instant visual/audio feedback

Impact: Ensures students are genuinely attentive, not just present in front of the screen.

🎤 AI Viva Trainer (Gemini-Powered)

StudyNest integrates Google Gemini to simulate viva and oral exams.

Features:

Generates subject-specific viva questions

Evaluates answers for clarity, structure, and conceptual depth

Provides instant feedback and scores

Impact: Transforms rote memorization into confident explanation and improves exam readiness.

🎮 Gamification & Rewards

All activities in StudyNest feed into a reward system.

Gamification Elements:

XP points

Levels and streaks

Performance-based rewards

Impact: Turns discipline into motivation and builds sustainable study habits.

🧠 Technology Stack

Frontend: React.js, Tailwind CSS

Computer Vision: TensorFlow, MediaPipe

AI Evaluation: Google Gemini

Audio & Notifications: Web Audio API, Browser Notifications

Architecture: Modular AI-driven learning ecosystem
